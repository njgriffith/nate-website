The difficulty of Celeste precedes itself, but this difficulty can see much greater heights in the world of modded Celeste. The biggest of which is called Strawberry Jam.

Strawberry Jam is a mod that is larger than the original game itself, and a mod that sees the game pushed to its absolute limits in terms of what is possible. There are 5 "lobbies" available in SJ, each lobby is a collection of levels. Each level is labeled either "green", "yellow", or "red" based on it's relative difficulty within the lobby. ex. a red beginner level is harder than a yellow beginner level is harder than a green beginner level, but a green intermediate level is harder than a red beginner level and so on.

After you complete every level in a lobby, you unlock that lobbies final and most difficult challenge called the "Heartside".

  1. Beginner lobby - Low to moderate difficulty compared to the base game
  2. Intermediate lobby - Moderate to hard difficulty compared to the base game
  3. Advanced lobby - Comparable to the very hardest levels in the base game and beyond
  4. Expert lobby - Much much harder than anything in the base game
  5. Grandmaster lobby - Adderall
