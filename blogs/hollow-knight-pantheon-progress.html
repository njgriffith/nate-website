In Hollow Knight, the pantheons are the final challenge of the game, and they are far and away the most difficult challenges available to you.

You can however, greatly increase this difficulty by equipping a binding. There are 4 to choose from.

  • Nail binding - Reduces the damage you deal with your nail (sword) from 21 to 13.
  • Shell binding - You now have 4 health instead of 9.
  • Soul binding - You soul capacity (for healing and casting spells) is reduced to 17% of the original.
  • Charm binding - You can no longer use charms. Charms give you bonuses like extra damage or healing.
The ultimate and final challenge is to have all bindings on at the same time.
